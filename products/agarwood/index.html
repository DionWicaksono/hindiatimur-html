Premium Grades

Three Tiers of
Oud Excellence

Each grade is independently verified by GC-MS analysis. Resin content, agarospirol, and patchoulol levels determine classification. All grades meet international export standards and CITES compliance.

01
Premium Grade
Resin Content: 5-8%

Entry-level export grade with balanced aroma complexity. Suitable for fragrance blending, incense, and wellness applications. Consistent quality for bulk buyers and private label partners.

02
Super-Premium Grade
Resin Content: 8-12%

High-resin agarwood with pronounced depth and longevity. Preferred by luxury fragrance houses and high-end oud traders. Superior agarospirol and patchoulol profiles for discerning buyers.

03
Ultra-Premium Grade
Resin Content: 12%+

Exceptional resin density with complex, multi-layered aroma. Limited availability. Reserved for premium fragrance compositions, bespoke oud blends, and collector-grade applications. Highest market value.

From Forest to Port

How We Deliver
Certified Quality

Every batch of agarwood passes through rigorous testing and compliance verification -- from forest sourcing to pre-shipment inspection. No shortcuts, full transparency.

01
Forest Sourcing

Agarwood is harvested from certified wild and cultivated sources in Aceh and Kalimantan. All sourcing complies with CITES regulations and local forestry laws.

02
Initial Sorting

Raw agarwood is cleaned, graded by resin content, and sorted by size and form. Initial quality assessment determines preliminary grade classification.

03
Lab Testing

Every batch undergoes GC-MS analysis at ISO 17025-accredited laboratories. Agarospirol, patchoulol, and resin content are quantified. Final grade is assigned.

04
Packaging & Docs

Agarwood is vacuum-sealed into foil or custom containers. CITES permits, certificates of origin, and lab reports are prepared for export.

05
Compliance Clearance

Final inspection by export team. All documentation verified. Container is sealed and ready for shipment to buyer's destination.

Common Questions

Buyer FAQ

Answers to the questions oud traders and fragrance importers ask most when sourcing premium agarwood from Indonesia.

What is the difference between the three grades?
The three grades are determined by resin content and GC-MS analysis. Premium (5-8% resin) is suitable for blending and bulk orders. Super-Premium (8-12% resin) offers higher agarospirol levels and deeper aroma. Ultra-Premium (12%+ resin) is reserved for luxury fragrances and collector-grade applications. All grades are independently lab-verified.
Is CITES documentation included?
Yes. Every shipment includes a valid CITES permit, certificate of origin, and all required export documentation. We handle all compliance requirements to ensure smooth customs clearance in your destination country.
What is the minimum order quantity?
The standard MOQ is 5 kg per grade. For trial orders or custom blends, we can accommodate smaller quantities on a case-by-case basis. Contact our team to discuss your specific requirements.
Can I request custom forms or sizes?
Yes. We can supply agarwood in resin chunks, chips, or powder form. Custom sizing and packaging are available for orders above 10 kg per grade. All custom requests are processed with a 4-6 week lead time.
What compliance documentation is provided?
Every shipment includes: CITES permit, GC-MS analysis report, certificate of origin, phytosanitary certificate, heavy metal test report, and ISO 17025 certificate of analysis. All documents are provided prior to shipment.
How long is the lead time?
Standard lead time is 3-6 weeks from order confirmation, depending on grade availability and testing requirements. We will confirm exact availability and lead time within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry.
