Common Questions

Buyer FAQ

Answers to common questions from international fresh produce importers.

What is the Mentega variety?
"Mentega" (Butter) is a premium Indonesian variety known for its thick, bright yellow flesh, high oil content, and exceptionally creamy texture. It is often preferred over Hass in Asian markets for its rich flavor profile.
How do you manage the cold chain?
Avocados are pre-cooled immediately after harvest and maintained at 5°C – 7°C throughout transport. We use temperature-controlled reefers for sea freight and insulated packaging for air freight.
What is the lead time for export?
For air freight, we can ship within 3-5 days of order confirmation. For sea freight, the lead time is typically 7-10 days to allow for harvesting, grading, and consolidation.
Can you provide ripeness control?
Yes. We ship "hard green" fruit to ensure maximum shelf life upon arrival. We can provide specific ripening protocols for your local distribution centers.
