From Farm to Port

How We Deliver
Consistent Quality

Every batch of fruit chips passes through the same five-stage quality chain -- from harvest scheduling to pre-shipment inspection. No shortcuts, no blind sourcing.

01
Harvest Scheduling

Partner farms are visited 2 weeks pre-harvest. Ripeness, pest-free status, and field hygiene are assessed before commitment.

02
Same-Day Processing

Fruit is transported to processing facility within 6 hours of harvest. Slicing, washing, and frying or drying begins immediately to preserve quality.

03
QC & Lab Testing

Every batch is tested for moisture, oil content, microbial load, heavy metals, and pesticide residue at ISO 17025-accredited laboratories.

04
Packing & Labelling

Packed into foil-lined bags with nitrogen flushing. Custom retail packaging and private label printing completed at this stage.

05
Pre-Shipment Inspection

Final inspection by our export team. Phytosanitary certificate, certificate of analysis, and all compliance documents prepared before container loading.

Common Questions

Buyer FAQ

Answers to the questions importers ask most when sourcing fruit chips from Indonesia for the first time.

What is the difference between vacuum-fried and air-dried chips?
Vacuum-fried chips are cooked at low temperature (100–120°C) under vacuum pressure, resulting in a crispy texture with relatively low oil absorption. Air-dried chips use no oil at all -- fruit is dehydrated at 50–60°C, making them ideal for clean-label and organic positioning. Air-dried chips have a chewier texture and higher nutrient retention.
What is the minimum order quantity?
The standard MOQ is 100 kg per variety for bulk orders. For OEM / private label and custom mixed blends, the minimum is 500 kg per SKU. We can accommodate trial orders at 50 kg for qualified buyers -- request this when submitting your quote.
Can I request custom flavours or seasoning?
Yes. Common seasoning additions include sea salt, coconut sugar glaze, chilli, cinnamon, and lime. We can also develop custom flavour profiles for private label buyers. Flavour development is included in the OEM package at MOQ 500 kg per variant. All flavourings use natural ingredients only unless otherwise specified.
Do you offer halal or organic certification?
Halal certification (MUI) is available on request for all varieties. Organic certification (IMO / USDA NOP) is available for pineapple and air-dried banana. Other varieties can be sourced from certified organic farms with 4–6 weeks additional lead time. Certification documents are provided prior to shipment.
What compliance documentation is provided?
Every shipment includes a Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin, Certificate of Analysis (ISO 17025), Heavy Metal Test Report, and Pesticide Residue Analysis. Nutritional declarations, allergen statements, and country-of-origin labelling can be provided for EU, US, or Middle East market requirements on request.
How long is the lead time from order to shipment?
Standard lead time is 2–4 weeks from order confirmation for in-season varieties. OEM and custom blend orders allow 4–6 weeks for product development, testing, and packaging. We will confirm exact availability and lead time within 24 hours of receiving your inquiry.
