Grade Reference

Every Grade,
Consistently Supplied

Indonesian cashews are traded under the AFMPC grading system. We stock the six most commercially active grades year-round and can source the full range on a contract basis.

W180
Jumbo Whole
≤ 180 kernels / lb · Largest Grade

The prestige grade. Maximum kernel size commands a significant premium. Suited to luxury gifting, premium retail packs, and upscale food service. Limited seasonal availability -- advise your volume early.

W240
Large Whole
181–240 kernels / lb · Premium

Strong retail positioning with consistent supply. The preferred grade for branded health snack products and gourmet nut mixes entering the EU, US, and Middle East markets.

W320
Standard Whole
241–320 kernels / lb · Most Traded

The global benchmark grade -- highest volume, most competitive pricing, widest buyer base. Available year-round in quantity. The default recommendation for first-time importers.

W450
Small Whole
321–450 kernels / lb · Bakery

Ideal for bakery applications, trail mixes, and ingredient blends where kernel size is less critical than cost. Full compliance documentation identical to premium grades.

SW
Splits
Natural halves · Value Grade

Kernels split naturally during processing. Full flavour, same nutritional profile as whole. Widely used in confectionery, granola bars, and trail mix at significantly lower cost per kilo.

LP/SP
Pieces
Large & small pieces · Ingredient

Pre-broken kernels for food manufacturing. Consistent piece size per grade. Used in nut butters, pralines, toppings, and ice cream inclusions. MOQ 500 kg, fastest lead time in the range.

From Orchard to Container

How We Deliver
Consistent Quality

Cashew quality is determined at five points between harvest and loading. We own the process at every stage -- no blind sourcing, no third-party aggregation without audit.

01
Farm Audit

Partner orchards are audited annually for pesticide usage, soil health, and harvest practice. Only audited farms supply into our export chain.

02
Shelling & Grading

Raw nuts are mechanically shelled and electronically graded at our HACCP-certified partner facility. Rejects are removed at machine level before human inspection.

03
Lab Testing

Every lot is tested for aflatoxin (≤10 ppb), heavy metals, pesticide residue, and moisture at ISO 17025-accredited laboratories before packing approval.

04
Vacuum Packing

Approved kernels are vacuum-sealed in 25 kg food-grade tins or nitrogen-flushed foil bags. Roasted variants are packed within 2 hours of processing.

05
Pre-Shipment Inspection

Final documentation package assembled: phytosanitary certificate, COA, aflatoxin report, certificate of origin, and packing list. Container loaded under team supervision.

Common Questions

Buyer FAQ

The questions importers ask most when sourcing Indonesian cashews for the first time.

How does Indonesian cashew compare to Vietnamese or Indian origin?
Indonesian cashew is grown in a distinct volcanic and tropical terroir that produces a slightly creamier kernel with lower shrinkage on roasting compared to some Vietnamese grades. While Vietnam dominates global volume, Indonesian origin commands a small premium in markets that value traceability and shorter supply chains to Southeast Asia. Our NTT-origin cashews are increasingly sought by EU buyers seeking non-Vietnam diversification.
What aflatoxin standard do you comply with?
We test to EU Regulation (EC) No 1881/2006 limits: total aflatoxin ≤ 10 ppb and aflatoxin B1 ≤ 5 ppb. Testing is performed at ISO 17025-accredited laboratories and the report is provided with every shipment. For US-bound shipments we also provide documentation to meet FDA / FSMA requirements on request.
Can I request a specific grade not listed here?
Yes. We can supply the full AFMPC grade range including W180, W210, W240, W280, W320, W450, and all split and piece grades (SW, SSW, LP, SP, BB, SB). W180 and W210 are seasonal -- contact us with your timeline and we will confirm availability and indicative pricing within 24 hours.
Do you offer halal or organic certification?
Halal certification (MUI) is available on request for all grades and processing types. Organic certification (IMO / USDA NOP) is available for select origin farms in East Nusa Tenggara with 4–6 weeks additional lead time for certification documentation to be prepared. Both can be included in the pre-shipment documentation package.
What is the minimum order and lead time?
Minimum order is 1 MT for whole kernel grades, and 500 kg for splits and pieces. Standard lead time is 1–3 weeks from order confirmation for in-stock grades. Roasted and flavoured variants add approximately one week. We recommend 4 weeks lead time for first orders to allow for documentation preparation and any buyer-specific requirements.
What packaging options are available?
Standard bulk export packaging is 25 kg vacuum-sealed tins or 10 kg nitrogen-flushed foil bags. For OEM and private label orders, we can produce retail-ready packaging from 100g consumer bags to 1 kg resealable pouches with buyer branding, nutritional declarations, and barcode integration. Packaging artwork and proofing is included in the OEM service at MOQ 2 MT.
