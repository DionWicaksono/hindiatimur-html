How It Works

From Initial Inquiry to Port Delivery

We've streamlined the entire sourcing process -- so you spend less time chasing paperwork and more time growing your business.

01
Inquiry & Scoping

Submit your product requirements, volume, grade specs, and target market. We respond within 24 hours.

02
Sample & Quotation

Receive physical product samples and a detailed commercial quote including all compliance and logistics costs.

03
Agreement & Production

Sign the supply agreement, confirm packaging and labeling specs, and we begin sourcing or production.

04
QC & Documentation

Quality inspection, lab testing, and preparation of all export documents: phytosanitary, CoA, CoO, packing list, and more.

05
Shipment & Tracking

Goods dispatched via your preferred Incoterm (FOB, CIF, DDP) with full tracking and post-shipment support.
