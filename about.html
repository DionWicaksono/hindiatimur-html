Who We Are

Bridging the Gap Between
Farm and Global Market

We are an Indonesian agricultural export company specialising in traceable, compliance-ready produce — from specialty coffee and vanilla to agarwood, patchouli oil, and beyond.

The founding idea was straightforward: Indonesia is one of the world's most biodiverse agricultural regions, producing commodities that are genuinely irreplaceable in global flavour, fragrance, and nutrition supply chains. But smallholder farmers rarely have direct access to the international buyers who need their products most.

Hindia Timur closes that gap. We work directly with farmer cooperatives and estate producers across the archipelago, handling quality control, certification, and logistics so that international importers receive export-grade produce with all the documentation their markets require — and farmers receive fair, stable prices without the burden of navigating export bureaucracy alone.

Every product in our catalog is traceable to its growing region. Every shipment leaves with full phytosanitary documentation. Every partner — buyer and farmer alike — receives a dedicated point of contact from day one.

01
Transparency as a foundation

GPS-tagged farm records, batch codes, and harvest logs are standard — not optional. Buyers can trace any product back to the farm that grew it.

02
Compliance built in, not bolted on

We operate within the regulatory frameworks of the EU, US FDA, Middle East, and East Asian markets. Your cargo clears customs the first time, every time.

03
Sustainability as a commitment

From CITES-compliant agarwood to certified organic moringa, we prioritise sourcing practices that protect the ecosystems that make Indonesian produce extraordinary.

04
Fair pricing that flows to the farm

By eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, more of the value paid by international buyers reaches the farmers who cultivate the product. That is how quality is sustained across seasons.

Indonesia is not just a source of commodities. It is a living catalogue of flavour, fragrance, and nourishment that took centuries to cultivate. Our job is to make sure the world can access it — and that the people who grew it benefit from it.

What We Stand For

The Values That Guide
Every Shipment

These aren't brand words. They're the criteria by which we select suppliers, reject lots, and build long-term partnerships.

Uncompromising Quality

Every lot is tested against published grade specifications before acceptance. Inconsistent batches are rejected before they reach a buyer — not after.

Full Traceability

We provide GPS-linked farm data, batch-specific certificates of analysis, and harvest documentation for every single order we fulfill.

Environmental Stewardship

We work with CITES permits for protected species, promote organic certification, and prioritise sourcing partnerships that preserve the ecosystems they depend on.

Farmer Partnership

Our producer network is not a vendor list — it is a long-term relationship. We invest in farmer certification, training, and market access as much as our own logistics capability.

Regulatory Expertise

Phytosanitary compliance, CITES permits, EFSA standards, US FDA registration, Halal and Organic certifications — our documentation team handles the full set, so yours doesn't have to.

Responsive Partnership

Every buyer and every farmer partner has a named account manager. Quotes are returned within 24 business hours. Problems are solved before they become shipment delays.
